Do not go gentle into that good night, Old age should burn and rave at close of day; Rage, rage against the dying of the light. - Dylan Thomas
Part 1

For this project, our goal was to further familiarize ourselves with Fusion 360. We were to follow a tutorial to create a stamp, then create a new stamp with changed aspects. Most of the problems I ran into while doing this project was moving around and using hotkeys in the Fusion 360 program since I am much more used to Unity and Blender. I chose the rubber grip as well as the granite surface because the "Paid" stamp feels like it would be used by an official.
