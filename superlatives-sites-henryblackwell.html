Parking Lot of Church on the Hill

After a long day of Starbucks is a coffee shop with a table and chair for customers to sit and enjoy their coffee. However, this store has only a table without chairs. Because of no chairs, I never see customers stay there to enjoy their drinks. Instead, most of them grabbed their drinks and left very quickly. This Starbucks store is referred to as a "grab-and-go" coffee shop. Starbucks's model business focused on providing a third place between work and home where customers could socialize, relax, and enjoy coffee. However, this Starbucks store function in a very different way from the business model of Starbucks. When I last visited this store, there were tables but no chairs. However, now, they also put away tables. So, this Starbucks store's model business absolutely 100% shifts into "grab-and-go," making them unique compared to other local stores.


“The coffee shop that everyone would rather leave than stay after receiving their order.”
